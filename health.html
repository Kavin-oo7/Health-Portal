Brain Tumor Overview

Brain tumors arise from abnormal brain cell growth. They can be benign or cancerous and cause symptoms like headaches, seizures, and cognitive impairments. Early detection via imaging is crucial.

Our AI-enhanced MRI screening model assists physicians by accurately identifying suspicious tumor areas, facilitating quicker and more reliable clinical decisions.

Pneumonia Overview

Pneumonia is an infection that inflames lung air sacs, causing cough, fever, and breathlessness. It is a serious illness particularly risky to young children and the elderly.

Our AI chest X-ray analysis helps medical teams quickly identify pneumonia cases for effective treatment, helping reduce complications and hospitalizations.
