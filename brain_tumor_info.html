About Brain Tumors

Brain tumors can be benign or malignant. Early detection plays a key role in effective treatment and recovery.

  • Common Symptoms: Headaches, nausea, seizures, vision problems.
  • Diagnosis: MRI scans analyzed using AI and radiologist review.
  • Treatment: Surgery, radiation therapy, or chemotherapy.
